The Water Loop

2018

35”x 77”

The project involved selecting several Proto-Architectures that I had created and deploying, aggregating, and utilizing them to create a speculative immersive environment. I combined four Proto-Architectures to construct a futuristic city named "Water Loop." The vision was to depict a future world where all natural water sources are contaminated, leaving no oceans, lakes, or rivers. In response, people designed a city with a continuous circulation of clean water. The goal was to replicate the natural flow of water within the city, providing citizens with easy access to clean water and enhancing the city's aesthetic beauty. Additionally, this artificial water flow restored the natural water ecosystem, ensuring a clean water supply for the inhabitants.
