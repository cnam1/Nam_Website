Touch

2018

PLA and Acrylic on Wood, 17"x 7"x 5"

Drawing inspiration from the work of Sophie Kahn, I aimed to convey the imperfection of technology through a glitch or fragmented effect. I 3D scanned my own hand. I then 3D printed the result and created two different hands. One of these hands I deliberately tore apart and reassembled to give it the appearance of an imperfectly rendered image.
