About

My name is Choonghyun Charlie Nam.

I am an interdisciplinary designer and artist currently studying at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. I have had the opportunity to study and continue my education in a wide range of creative fields, including architecture, photography, sculpture, film, and art and technology.

I am particularly captivated by the visual aesthetics of geometry, which serve as the driving force behind most of my creative process. Much of my work revolves around the creation of simple yet abstract art and design that viewers can easily observe and understand.
