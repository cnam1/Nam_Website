Architecture Collage: Chicago

2023

The Architecture Collage project was initially started as a simple collage exercise, but it quickly evolved into its own series. Inspired by the rich architectural diversity of the city of Chicago, I aimed to transform these familiar facades into a unique and mesmerizing experience by creating a new type of space through the selection of images of architectural facades and arranging them according to geometric principles.
